# 19th Floor 2BHK Apartment - Detailed Description

## Executive Summary and General Overview

This document provides a comprehensive architectural and interior description of a two-bedroom, hall, and kitchen (2BHK) residential flat situated on the 19th floor of a modern high-rise apartment complex. Because of its elevated position, the apartment naturally benefits from excellent ambient light, reduced street-level noise, and expansive aerial views of the surrounding urban landscape. The overall floor plan is meticulously designed to optimize space, utility, and natural ventilation.

The structural layout comprises a primary living room, an open-concept kitchen combined with a dining space, a master bedroom with an attached en-suite washroom, a secondary bedroom featuring an attached west-facing balcony, and one easily accessible common washroom. The design philosophy blends functional modern living with cozy, personalized aesthetics, ensuring every square foot is utilized effectively.

## Entrance and Living Room (8 x 14 Feet)

Upon entering, one steps directly into the primary living room, which serves as the central hub of the apartment. The living room occupies a rectangular footprint, measuring exactly 8 feet in width by 14 feet in length. This proportionate space is thoughtfully arranged to welcome guests and facilitate daily relaxation.

Immediately near the entryway, there is a dedicated shoe storage solution: a tall shoe case specifically designed to accommodate a large collection, with storage capacity for approximately 30 pairs of shoes. Its placement near the door ensures that the home remains clean and organized, preventing outdoor dirt from tracking inside.

The focal point of the seating arrangement is a striking blue sofa that adds a vibrant splash of color to the room, anchoring the space with a calm, inviting energy. Positioned directly in front is a small, functional center table, ideal for resting beverages, books, or decorative items.

Beneath the seating area lies a prominent, custom-sized carpet measuring exactly 5 feet by 13 feet. Given the living room's dimensions of 8x14 feet, this 5x13 feet carpet covers the vast majority of the floor length, adding warmth, acoustic dampening, and textural contrast to the hard flooring beneath.

## Open Kitchen and Dining Space

Proceeding seamlessly from the living area—without partition walls—one enters the expansive open kitchen and dining zone. The architectural choice to use an open kitchen concept significantly enhances the perceived volume of the apartment, allowing light and conversation to flow effortlessly between the living room and the culinary space.

The kitchen is notably large, providing ample room for meal preparation, storage, and movement. It features a modern layout that accommodates multiple appliances and extensive countertop space. Integrated directly into this large kitchen area is a dedicated dining space. This "kitchen cum dining" arrangement is highly social, allowing family members or guests to interact with whoever is cooking. The open nature of this space ensures that the entire public wing of the apartment feels airy, interconnected, and breathable.

## Master Bedroom and Attached Washroom

The private quarters are distinctly separated from the public living and dining areas. The primary suite—the Master Bedroom—is designed as a self-contained sanctuary for rest and productivity.

Central to the master bedroom is a comfortable main bed. Recognizing the modern necessity of remote work, the room has been strategically outfitted with a dedicated work desk and an accompanying ergonomic chair. This allows seamless transition between a restful environment and a focused home-office setup.

A defining aesthetic feature is a distinctively green-colored almirah (wardrobe). This green almirah not only provides essential storage for clothing, linens, and personal effects but also introduces a unique, earthy color palette to the room, contrasting beautifully with the rest of the decor.

Furthermore, the master bedroom features the luxury of an attached washroom (en-suite). This private bathroom is equipped with standard modern sanitary ware, offering complete privacy and convenience to the primary occupants without needing to step out into the common hallways.

## Second Bedroom and West-Facing Balcony

The secondary bedroom serves as a highly versatile space, equally capable of functioning as a guest room, a child's room, or an additional home office. Like the master suite, this second bedroom is furnished with its own comfortable bed and a separate work desk, ensuring that multiple occupants can engage in focused work or study simultaneously.

The most magnificent feature of the second bedroom is its direct access to the apartment's sole balcony. A glass door opens up to a small, private balcony specifically oriented to face the west. Because the apartment is located on the 19th floor, this west-facing balcony offers unobstructed, panoramic views of the sky. The western orientation means this space captures direct afternoon sunlight and provides a spectacular, front-row seat to golden hour and evening sunsets. The high altitude guarantees a steady cross-breeze, making the balcony an ideal spot for morning coffee or evening relaxation while overlooking the bustling city below.

## Common Washroom and Utilities

To serve the living room, kitchen, and the second bedroom, the apartment includes a centrally located common washroom. This washroom is easily accessible from the main living spaces, ensuring that visiting guests do not need to intrude into the private master suite. It is designed with standard wet and dry zones, complementing the attached washroom in the master bedroom to ensure that a fully occupied apartment experiences no bottlenecks during busy morning routines.

- **Carpet Dimensions**: 5 feet × 13 feet

- **Master Bedroom Features**: Main bed, work desk, ergonomic chair, green-colored almirah, attached en-suite washroom
- **Second Bedroom Features**: Bed, work desk, direct access to balcony

- **Key Advantages**: Excellent natural light, reduced noise due to elevation, panoramic urban views, modern open-plan design, dual work desks for remote work, privacy with two separated washrooms
